    Procedure:
  • Enter a binary message by clicking the bits and click on Submit.
  • The entered message will be displayed in Observations.
  • Enter the encoded codeword corresponding to the message and click on submit.
  • The correctness of the entered codeword will be displayed in Observations.
  • If the answer is correct, we will proceed to the next step. Otherwise, click on Reset to try these steps again with a different binary message.
  • Enter the number of errors you want to introduce in the transmitted codeword and click on Transmit.
  • The transmitted codeword will be displayed in Observations.
  • Enter the result of the minimum distance decoding and click on Check.
